Searching PNCA Library Resources Search Scopes

The catalog has several collections, or filters (search scopes), that allow a user to search specific combinations of our collections.

WU Libraries

Search for materials held by all Willamette University Libraries (including PNCA).

WU Libraries & Summit

Search for materials held by Willamette University Libraries and Summit.

Course Reserves

Use the Course Reserves option under Library Resources for items on reserve. You may search by instructor name or by title.
